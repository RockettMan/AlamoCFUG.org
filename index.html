Join The Alamo Area ColdFusion User Group Meetup to always be informed of the next meeting

This is a User Group focused on Rich Internet Application (RIA) development with languages such as ColdFusion, Flex, and AIR. Generally we cover programming topics more so than design topics. We also focus on such topics as object-oriented design, frameworks, and other ways to improve your development process.

We cover a variety of topics and skill levels, so please join us!

  • May 10th is our next meeting.
    Doug Boude will give us a preview of his topic Object Oriented Terminology for The Rest of Us. Doug will be presenting this topic at the Designer/Devloper Workflow Conference in June.

    RSVP at our Meetup site to earn an extra ticket in the drawing
  • Our normal meetings are once a month, on the second Monday of the month from 6:00 pm to 8:00 pm at the New Horizons Learning Center
  • We have cool raffle items and software giveaways several times a year!
  • We usually have pizza and snacks to enjoy while we talk and learn.
  • All are welcome, please join us.
