This is a User Group focused on Rich Internet Application (RIA) development with languages such as ColdFusion, Flex, and AIR. Generally we cover programming topics more so than design topics. We also focus on such topics as object-oriented design, frameworks, and other ways to improve your development process.

We cover a variety of topics and skill levels, so please join us!

  • June 23rd is a special Adobe Event. Come join us at the Igo Branch of the San Antonio Public Library where Adobe's Terry Ryan (Adobe Platform Evangelist) will be showing us the future of ColdFusion and Flex and some great things to come in the next product releases. As always, we'll have food, drinks and amazing giveaways worth over $2500. Come join us for some food, fun, and learning. RSVP at our Meetup site.
  • Our normal meetings are once a month, on the first Tuesday of the month from 6:00 pm to 8:00 pm at the New Horizons Learning Center
