Our group has just gotten off the ground. We've had three meetings so far with many opinions as to where the group should be headed and exactly how we should get there. We're all excited about the possibilities of a ColdFusion user group in San Antonio.

  • Congratulations to Eddie McDaniel ! He was our "BIG Prize" winner for December.
  • We meet once a month, on the first Tuesday of the month from 6:00 pm to 8:00 pm at Harcourt Assessment
  • Since the first Tuesday of January is New Years Day, we will change the meeting date to the third Tuesday, January 15th, only for that month.
  • Adobe is having a special meeting January 29th to Kick-off the launch of Flex 3 and AIR. We're meeting with our sister group the Alamo Area Mulit-Media User Group at the New Horizons Learning Center to learn about the new products, enjoy the food and win great prizes.
